Chris Young, Head of Sustainability and Co-Founder of Circumference Skincare Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Chris Young, Head of Sustainability and Co-Founder of Circumference Skincare. Chris has a long history working in the fashion and media industries before getting into skincare. Circumference is a brand that Chris started with his wife because from his standpoint, he didn’t know much about skincare and the more research he did he found that sustainable
skincare is few and far between.

The brand focuses on effective and sustainable products that can cater to everyone. The ingredients they use in their formulas are high quality, which makes the process longer but the end product even more worth it.
