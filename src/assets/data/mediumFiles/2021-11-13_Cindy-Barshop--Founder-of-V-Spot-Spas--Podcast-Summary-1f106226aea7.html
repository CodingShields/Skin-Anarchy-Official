Cindy Barshop, Founder of V Spot Spas, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Cindy Barshop, the Founder of V Spot Spas. Cindy has over twenty
years of experience in Women’s Healthcare. She is a nationally recognized and successful
entrepreneur and has also appeared on Bravo’s hit reality show, The Real Housewives of New York City. Her passion for Women’s Healthcare sparked when she had just had a baby and she found herself with urinary issues and a low libido and she researched about it and realized that there’s not enough innovation for women’s sexual health. “The VSPOT offers intimate, transformative, and medical vaginal healing treatments you didn’t know there were solutions for. The discomfort, embarrassment, perhaps even shame you might be feeling for a variety of reasons –dryness, inability to orgasm, or urinary incontinence painful sex; are solvable conditions performed with non-invasive, non-surgical pain-free, FDA-approved treatments. We focus on all the normal stages of a woman’s life, pregnancies, the natural aging process, menstruation, and menopause. At VSPOT we are interested in addressing, correcting, and preventing a single concern or several issues.” (vspotmedispa.com).
