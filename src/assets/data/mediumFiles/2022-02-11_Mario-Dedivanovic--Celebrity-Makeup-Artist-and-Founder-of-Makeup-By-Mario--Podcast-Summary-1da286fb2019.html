Mario Dedivanovic, Celebrity Makeup Artist and Founder of Makeup
By Mario, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Mario Dedivanovic, a Celebrity Makeup Artist and Founder of Makeup By Mario. Mario Dedivanovic is a well known name in the beauty industry. Mario got his start by working at Sephora at 17 years old, he started in the Fragrance department but was always drawn to the artistry of makeup, he would play around with it and show people certain things that
he thought could work for them and he fell in love with it after that. “From the very first day of working there I was naturally more attracted to makeup artistry.” (Dedivanovic). Mario was raised in Bronx, NY and comes from an immigrant household, to this day all he’s ever wanted to do was make them proud and he sure has with his success and his makeup line, Makeup by
Mario.“I have a very architectural approach, what gets me super inspired is looking at the symmetry of the face and analyzing the face. How can I perfect this, how can I lift this, how can I lower this, how can I cinch this. That’s the aspect of makeup artistry that I really love.” (Dedivanovic). Mario is best known for his makeup line, Makeup by Mario, as well as his makeup artistry on so many well known celebrities. His most famous and well known muse is
Kim Kardashian West. He talks about the importance of highlighting the face’s natural beauty and the best features on someone when doing their makeup.
