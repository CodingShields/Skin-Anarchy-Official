Michelle Roque-O’Neil, Founder of Therapie Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Michelle Roque-O’Neil, The Founder of Therapie. O’Neil has over forty years experience and is a spiritual health and wellness expert with a rare intuition and gift for healing. She became a Reiki Master about twenty years ago and is a leading force in Aromatherapy. O’Neil wanted to create something that people could use to self-care and heal on their own and use products that are effective and easy to use. She has always been inspired to make products that help people and is fascinated by natural and earthly ingredients. “a trained reflexologist, a sound and cranio-sacral therapist who has studied Native American rituals, numerology, shamanism, Taoist Qi Gong, SRT and the alchemy of essences. Michelle offers one of the most spiritually profound and life- changing healing experiences currently available, globally, full stop. One client described a treatment with her as ‘like going on a retreat’.”(Therapie website, roquesoneil.com)
