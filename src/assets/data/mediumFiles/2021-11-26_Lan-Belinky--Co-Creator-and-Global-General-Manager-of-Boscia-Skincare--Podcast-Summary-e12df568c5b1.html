Lan Belinky, Co-Creator and Global General Manager of Boscia
Skincare, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Lan Belinky, the Co-Creator and Global General Manager of Boscia Skincare. Boscia stands for Botanical Science and they are a leading brand in clean beauty and have been for a while. Lan was born and raised in Japan but moved to the states in her youth because her father, the Founder of Boscia Skincare, Gen Inomata, wanted to bring the Japanese secrets and methods of skincare to the U.S. Japan is known informally as the Skincare/Medical center of the world in terms of innovation. Boscia pledges to be a clean beauty brand, their products include Cruelty-Free, Vegan, Artificial Fragrance and Color Free ingredients. “Clean
skincare doesn’t have to be boring in order to be effective. At boscia, we introduce innovative formats and textures to bring excitement to your daily routine because I believe skin care should be nontoxic, effective and FUN! Sticking true to its values of remaining vegan, cruelty-free and formulating for even the most sensitive skin types, I’m proud boscia has been a pioneer in clean beauty since 2002.” (boscia.com).
