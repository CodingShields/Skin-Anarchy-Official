Leslie Harris, Global General Manager of SkinCeuticals Podcast Summary

Ekta talks to Leslie Harris, The Global General Manager of SkinCeuticals. SkinCeuticals is a L’Oreal owned skincare brand founded on antioxidant science. Antioxidant science is the work that antioxidants do in products to prevent damage such as UV rays and other environmental factors. This company embraces different cultures and backgrounds and Leslie talks about all the hard work that goes into taking a brand global. The knowledge and science behind catering a brand or a group of products to a different culture or country or region takes a lot of careful consideration and work to understand what will work best for that group of people. What works
for one group of people might not work for another group and vice versa.

Leslie talks about her rise to where she is now and mentions a previous Skincare Anarchy guest, Marie-Pierre Stark-Flora, Founder of HERB + FLORA, was her first boss at L’Oreal. Harris learned a lot on the job
and went back to school for her Masters in Professional Studies in Cosmetics & Fragrance, Marketing & Management at FIT. On top of all she does now, she also is a professor at FIT, teaching a class on the history of beauty.
