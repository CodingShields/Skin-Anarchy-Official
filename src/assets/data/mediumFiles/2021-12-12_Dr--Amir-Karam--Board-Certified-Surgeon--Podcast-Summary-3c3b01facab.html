Dr. Amir Karam, Board Certified Surgeon, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Dr. Amir Karam, A double board-certified surgeon by the American
Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and American Board of Otolaryngology
(head/neck surgery) and one of the top plastic surgeons in San Diego. Dr. Karam has done so
much with his career, with 20+ years of experience, he is driven by the genuine desire to improve
his patients’ lives through transformative procedures that are both cosmetically and functionally enhancing. Surgery became the route Amir wanted to take because his mother had a congenital
heart condition and eventually went through a heart transplant. He is the creator of Karam MD Skincare, a skincare line focused on affordability and results. “When we say “simple and effective”, we mean it. Formulated with a series of active ingredients that will brighten your skin to leave you glowing, the Karam Trifecta does the work of a series of products that would
normally cost you three to four times as much. Our goal, like our skincare, is simple. We believe that skincare should be affordable, easy and accessible because everyone deserves to both look and feel their very best every single day.” (karammdskin.com).
