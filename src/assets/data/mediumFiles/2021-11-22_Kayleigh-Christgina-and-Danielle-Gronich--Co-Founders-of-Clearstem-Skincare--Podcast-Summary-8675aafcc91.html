Kayleigh Christgina and Danielle Gronich, Co-Founders of Clearstem Skincare, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Kayleigh Christgina and Danielle Gronich, the Co-Founders of Clearstem Skincare. Clearstem Skincare is a non-toxic skincare line that targets acne, anti-aging and scar reversing by utilizing premium ingredients. Danielle’s way into the skincare world was by way of dealing with cystic acne for around ten years before going back to school and earning
her license. She has a background in biology and it pushed her to figure out how to treat her skin on her own, which eventually led her to opening a clinic for patients that have tried the traditional dermatology route and had no other options. Her trials at her clinic have a 98% success rate of clearing the patient’s skin and that’s where she met her partner, Kayleigh.
Kayleigh is a holistic nutritionist that also suffered from painful cystic acne. The two girls teamed up to create their skincare brand that is focused on helping those who have lost hope in skincare.
