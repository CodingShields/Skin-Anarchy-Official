Bradley and Jonina Skaggs, Co-Founders of Skaggs Creative, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Bradley and Jonina Skaggs, Co-Founders of Skaggs Creative. Skaggs Creative is a Branding and Digital Company. They both have Architectural backgrounds. Jonina has a background in Art and Designing which has come in handy in their company when creating branding for companies. Their company understands the specific requirements of effective
branding for a diverse range of businesses, including high-tech, hospitality, fashion, beauty,
fragrance, etc. Jonina firmly believes that perspective and proportion are equally important when
creating a design and she focuses on the big things and the small alike, every piece matters. Jonina is also an educator who is passionate about teaching marketing and communications at the University of Iceland, and branding and packaging at the College of Arts and Crafts in Northern Ireland. Bradley has worked on several urban planning and architectural projects in Germany and Luxembourg and has had a great time merging all of his talents with Jonina’s and creating this empire.
