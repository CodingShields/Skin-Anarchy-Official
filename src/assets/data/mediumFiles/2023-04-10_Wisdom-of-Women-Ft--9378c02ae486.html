Wisdom of Women Ft. Liana Zavo — Forbes Thought Leader, Author, Leading Lady of PR & Branding Expert — E.435

In this inspiring episode of Skincare Anarchy, host Dr. Ekta interviews Liana Zavo, a Forbes Thought Leader, author, and branding expert who has made a significant impact in the world of PR and marketing. The conversation delves into Liana’s experiences, her insights on women’s empowerment, and her perspectives on branding and marketing in the skincare industry.

Dr. Ekta begins the discussion by exploring Liana Zavo’s background, her journey in the world of PR, marketing, and branding, and her passion for empowering women. They talk about Liana’s unique approach, which focuses on helping women build their personal and professional brands, making a lasting impact in their respective industries.

Throughout the conversation, Liana provides valuable insights into the importance of personal branding and how it can lead to success in the skincare industry and beyond. She emphasizes the need for authenticity and storytelling when building a brand, and how these elements can create a strong emotional connection with consumers.

Liana shares her thoughts, stating,

“In today’s competitive market, authenticity and storytelling are essential for building a powerful brand. It’s about creating an emotional connection with your audience and showcasing your unique value proposition.”

The host and guest also discuss the challenges and opportunities faced by women in the PR and marketing fields, as well as the importance of mentorship and support in fostering women’s growth and success. They highlight the role of networks and communities in providing guidance, resources, and encouragement for women pursuing their dreams.

Liana further elaborates,

“Supporting and uplifting fellow women is crucial for our collective growth and success. By building strong networks and communities, we can empower one another to break barriers and achieve our goals.”

Furthermore, the conversation explores Liana’s experiences as an author and her perspectives on the power of written words in shaping one’s personal brand. She shares insights into her writing process, her inspirations, and the role that writing plays in her career and personal life.

Reflecting on her writing journey, Liana remarks,

“Writing has been an incredible way for me to express my thoughts, share my experiences, and connect with people on a deeper level. It’s a powerful tool that allows me to impact and inspire others.”

By offering an in-depth look into the wisdom of women and the power of personal branding, this episode of Skincare Anarchy with Dr. Ekta provides valuable insights for anyone interested in the world of PR, marketing, and branding, particularly in the skincare industry. Through the inspiring story and expertise of Liana Zavo, listeners can learn about the importance of authenticity, storytelling, and women’s empowerment in building a successful brand and career.
