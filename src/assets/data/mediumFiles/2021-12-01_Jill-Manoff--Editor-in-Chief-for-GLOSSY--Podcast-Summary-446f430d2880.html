Jill Manoff, Editor in Chief for GLOSSY, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Jill Manoff, the Editor-in-Chief for GLOSSY. GLOSSY is a publication that explores the transformation of fashion, luxury, beauty and retail industry through technology
and the digital age. Manoff is from St. Louis, Missouri, she’s been obsessed with fashion and
beauty for her entire life and always thought a career in this industry was so farfetched. She
graduated with a dual degree in Psychology and Management, and took the first job she could,
which was an accounting executive at a local company for about two years before finding a magazine called Alive, which at the time was a fitness and nutrition magazine but were looking to hire someone to expand them into the fashion industry. Manoff says herself she didn’t have too much experience in writing but she took this freelance job as a stepping stone to where she is now. She is a very accomplished writer and found her calling into editing.
