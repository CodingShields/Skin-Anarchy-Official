Tammy Fender, Founder of Tammy Fender Holistic Skin Care and
Industry Veteran, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Tammy Fender, Founder of Tammy Fender Holistic Skin Care and Industry Veteran. Tammy’s skincare line is very plant and naturally based, she has discovered a love for plants and all things natural when she was a child. “It’s been a calling since childhood, without even realizing it. As a child, I always felt very connected to plants, I was very
comfortable. Whereas other kids might’ve been running off and playing sports…I always felt that there was something magical about the plant kingdom.” (Fender). Tammy has had a wonderful career and has always had a knack for helping others. All of her products are so near to her heart and you can tell by the way she speaks about her products and her brand that this
company is so important to her. She speaks about her journey, becoming an esthetician and working with essential oils and plant based products, as well as her passion for this line. “What I would like to do is become an esthetician, because I felt if I could really learn and understand the skin, then I could understand products.” (Fender).
