Erno Laszlo Explains the Mind & Skin Connection with New Podcast Ft. Poppy Jamie, Ambassador of Erno Laszlo, Podcast Summary

This Sunday, Ekta sits down with Poppy Jamie, Mental Health Researcher, Author, and Global Wellness Advisor to Erno Laszlo, to discuss her philosophical and revolutionary view of the health and wellness of the skin and mind as one entity. Poppy’s fascinating study and research of mental health and its relation to our skin allowed her to see that skin care depends on the body’s overall state.

“To understand skincare in a broad way is important. Getting a goodnights’ sleep or hydrating as well can also be considered as skincare.” (Jamie).

Poppy fell in love with the Erno Laszlo brand because of its products that give beautiful results and its willingness to innovate the idea of skin care and health within the beauty industry.

“I really appreciate what Erno Laszlo has done because they are a brand that creates beautiful and very effective products. But they are also wanting to make sure that their community understands that they support a full 360 approach to skincare, a holistic approach.” (Jamie).

Poppy understands that the world of consumerism, especially beauty, is drastically changing, and clients want more out of their skincare products than only a brand name. They want the scientific research to back up the results, and Erno Laszlo is doing just that!

“I think more than ever people are really intelligent and not gullible, they want to know what is in their skincare, what their body is absorbing, how the science of skincare works, etc. That is also a big reason I have chosen to work with Erno Laszlo because they have over 100 years of data.” (Jamie).
