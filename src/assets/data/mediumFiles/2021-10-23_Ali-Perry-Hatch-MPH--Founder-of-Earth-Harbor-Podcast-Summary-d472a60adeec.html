Ali Perry-Hatch MPH, Founder of Earth Harbor Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Ali Perry-Hatch MPH, The Founder of Earth Harbor Skincare. Hatch dives deep into her history and talks about her growing career in humanitarian engineering and graduate research in environmental toxicology. Hatch got into a near fatal car accident and after finding out she was pregnant which caused her to really take into account what she was putting on and inside her body. Hatch is very passionate about humanity and the newer generations that she wanted to create a line that encapsulated her personality yet made the consumers feel good. She speaks about how she was in a dark place when she started this line and the packaging of her products really helped her get creative again and express herself. She is a very spiritual person and hopes to share her positive view with the world through Earth Harbor Skincare.
