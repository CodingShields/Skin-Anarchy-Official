Indie Lee, Founder of Indie Lee Skincare Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Indie Lee, the Founder of Indie Lee Skincare. Lee was diagnosed with a brain tumor in 2008 and her doctor suggested it could have to do with what she’s putting in her body or on her body. She had been very healthy in terms of food and nutrition so she started to think about what she was putting on her skin. Her doctor told her that they had started to see more and more illnesses related to skincare and the environment which led Indie to begin her skincare journey as well as her recovery process, she had surgery and is cancer free to this day. She wanted to create something so clean and environmentally friendly to prevent what had happened to her, happening to others. Her collection and brand is all about empowering and
inspiring others to pay attention to what they use and learn about harmful chemicals and their reaction to your body.
