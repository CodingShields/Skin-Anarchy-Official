Chloe Kwak, Co-Founder & Co-CEO of 107 Beauty, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta sits down with Chloe Kwak, Co-Founder & Co-CEO of 107 Beauty, to discuss all avenues of beauty and skincare. Chole expresses her beautiful vision of 107 and implores that every product meets specific requirements before hitting any shelf! Chole and the 107-team brand themselves on producing quality, efficient products and gearing away from the trend-setting movement.

“I have experience in the global cosmetic industry that spanning over 17 years. I have always been passionate and interested in skincare not just because of my job but also because of my skin problems that I have had in the past. I started having skin problems in my 30s. I had to move around due to my job and had to adapt to new environments and that’s when my eczema started.” (Kwak).

Chloe knows what a consumer’s experience is like when it comes to struggling with our skincare and finding products that work for us and our daily lives. It was this exact reason that she wanted to pursue not only a path to beauty but transform the idea of skin care products and their purpose.

“Because of my eczema problem I got my esthetician license during that time because I really wanted to know more about my skin, and I wanted to find the solution!” (Kwak).

After meeting her founding partner Seyoung Shin, they broke down the root of several skin issues. As a result, they created a line formulated to not only conquer but rejuvenate the skin with natural ingredients such as their signature vinegar ingredient. From this dynamic duo, we have the extraordinary brand 107 Beauty!

“After I met Seyoung Shin, who is my partner and co-founder of 107 and I got to use this vinegar that is the core ingredient to several of our products it really changed my life. So, I wanted to share the benefits I experienced with this special vinegar. So, Seyoung and I created 107 to share the benefits!” (Kwak).
