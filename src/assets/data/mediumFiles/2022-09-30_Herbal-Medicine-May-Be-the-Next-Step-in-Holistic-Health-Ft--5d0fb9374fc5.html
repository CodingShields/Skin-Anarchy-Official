Herbal Medicine May Be the Next Step in Holistic Health Ft. Barbara Close, Founder of NATUROPATHICA, Podcast Summary

In this episode, Ekta sits down with Barbara Close, Founder of NATUROPATHICA, to discuss her journey and background in herbal medicine that inspired the incredible brand that protects and heals the skin but the entire body!

“I was not the girl who played with her mother’s jar of ‘Olay’ that’s for one thing. I was also not the girl who had chronic eczema and vowed to find a cure. I found the world of skin and beauty by accident.” (Close).

Although Barbara does not have the typical founding story, who does? Her passion and drive to understand and promote skincare health and wellness moved the NATUROPATHICA brand to where it is today.

“When I founded NATUROPATHICA, a healing arts center was my vision. The skincare part of that, when I started getting more interested in looking at different aspects of wellness, and of course skincare is part of it.” (Close).

Barbara’s love for skin health and wellness continues to grow, and NATUROPATHICA is a brand that cuts the lines of holistic health and beauty!

“It (the beauty space) collided with my love for holistic health and my passion for wellness rituals.” (Close).
