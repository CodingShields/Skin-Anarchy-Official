Jan Marini is Back to Explain Her Iconic Skin Care Management System, Podcast Summary

In this episode, Ekta sits down again with the brilliant Jan Marini as a continuation of our series. She discusses the ins and outs of skincare from the perspective of science. But even further into her skincare practices which grants her the title of The Skincare Queen!

“When I talk to the media and they interview me, I know at least two of the questions they’re going to ask me. And one is ‘Do I use my own products,’ and yes, I do. And the second is, ‘why do I do all the research and development and what inspires me to read all the science journals on products’, and I give the same answer every single time. I do it because I’m selfish!” (Marini).

We all need to be more selfish as consumers, especially in skincare, because so many brands bypass products that not only show no results but cause more harm. Jan takes the time to understand the science behind products not only because of her background but her determination to find the cleanest and most effective products for the skin!
