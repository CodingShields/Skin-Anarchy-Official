Marie-Pierre Stark-Flora, Founder of HERB + FLORA Podcast Summary

Air Date: 09/07/2021

On this week’s podcast, Ekta talks to Marie-Pierre Stark-Flora (the founder of HERB + FLORA, a gender-neutral, clean and vegan skincare brand) about her experience in the luxury beauty world and her work with brands such as Khiel’s, L’Oréal, Estée Lauder and many other brands. Stark-Flora speaks about her athletic experience as well, she is a triathlete and talks about the importance of fitness and the role it plays in our overall health, including skincare. She mentions a Canadian doctor who says that exercise has an anti-aging effect on skin, and that he has been able to create a product that is close to replicating that same effect on the skin.

Stark-Flora goes on to talk about creating a topical formula as well as ingestible products that help with anti-aging as well as a pillowcase made with copper irons that accelerates production of new cells in the skin which contributes to the desired effect. Marie’s vision for the future of her brand is big and bright as she talks about how she wants to expand her line, dive into more specialized products for different skin types, and hair care down the line. Marie says towards the end of the podcast “Do not take failure as a failure” which is a wonderful piece of advice to end this podcast on.
