Luxury Lashes Made Easy for Everyone Ft. Haley & Zach Chipman, Founders of PRO LASH, Podcast Summary

In this episode, Ekta sits with Haley and Zack Chipman, founders of PRO LASH, to discuss their outstanding success as a brand by providing professional-grade lashes for the average consumer.

“As we have been doing professional eyelash extensions, we found that there’s one problem. A lot of women don’t have time still to get their eyelashes done” (The Chipmans).

Although these lashes are a true gem of beauty engineering, they did take much time for the team to create at a pace that would satisfy consumer needs.

“We were trying for 4 years to figure out the time problem. We were like, ‘How do we address this? How do we get these lashes on everyone? They’re amazing, women will love them.’ We started with our engineers, who were trying to create them faster and then we created PROLASH.” (The Chipmans).

From there, Haley and Zack are about to team up with their team and formulate a way for consumers to have a lash extension of high quality and faster delivery to keep up with the hassle of their daily life!
