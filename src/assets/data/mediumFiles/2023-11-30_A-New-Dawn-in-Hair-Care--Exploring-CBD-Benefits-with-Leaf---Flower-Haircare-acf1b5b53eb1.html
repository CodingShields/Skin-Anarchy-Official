A New Dawn in Hair Care: Exploring CBD Benefits with Leaf & Flower Haircare

Leaf and Flower is a hair care brand launched in 2019. The brand is driven by the founder’s passion for CBD and its potential benefits for hair and scalp health. The brand’s head of marketing, Stacey Kilfoy, explains the brand was built upon extensive research and scientific proof. In addition, the team discovered over 116 different cannabinoids that offer remarkable benefits to the hair, such as hair growth and addressing various scalp issues.

The brand’s team of chemists found that our bodies have endocannabinoid receptors that can interact with CBD — taken orally or applied topically. By combining three key components of cannabinoids under high pressure and low temps, they create an effect that promotes natural hair growth and stronger and healthier hair.

Stacey goes on to emphasize the importance of scalp health and why Leaf and Flower aimed to create a solution to provide clients with products for at-home care. The products are created for consumers who want to see immediate results. Furthermore, Leaf and Flower products address
the weak points of curly hair — nurturing healthy curls. Customers have reported noticeable improvements in the quality of their hair after using Leaf and Flower products. The brand’s line includes three collections: the Crow Collection, Volume Collection, and Instant Damage Correction Collection. Each of these targets specific hair concerns and tailored solutions for diverse needs. Above all, with a focus on scalp health and nourishment, Leaf and Flower deliver stronger, fuller, and healthier hair.
