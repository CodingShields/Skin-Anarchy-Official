Stem Cell Skincare Is Finally Being Done Correctly Ft. Dr. Mitch Goldman of Calecim

The professional medical advisory board for CALECIM® has elected Dr. Goldman as its chairman. As a result, he is touring Asia giving lectures. In 1986, Mitchel P. Goldman, M.D., began his medical career. He is a highly regarded physician and cosmetic surgeon who holds dual certification from the American Board of Dermatology and the American Board of Cosmetic Surgery. Dr. Goldman, a graduate of Stanford University Medical School and Boston University, is renowned for his groundbreaking work in the field of laser research.

He founded and served as the medical director of Cosmetic Laser Dermatology, a volunteer clinical dermatology at the University of California, San Diego, and an honorary professor of dermatology for the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences.

Here is what he has to say:

“Not only do something that’s phenomenal for people with ulcers but take those same growth factors and put it into a cream to stimulate the rejuvenation of the skin. I believe that growth factors we can obtain from the umbilical cord lining mesenchymal stem cells are much more effective in rejuvenating the skin, and that’s the Calecim product” (Goldman).

“If we want to enhance your beauty, it’s not a bad thing because enhancing someone’s beauty is basically turning the clock back a little bit, and if we continue turning the clock back, we can have people look as good as they feel because modern medicine is pretty amazing with our ability to keep people really healthy until they finally aren’t” (Goldman).

“All of the Calecim products are made with good manufacturing processes, they have no bacteria in them, so they really do have a long shelf life” (Goldman).

“We’re doing the same model we did with SkinMedica. We are basically doing the science, the research, and publications” (Goldman).

“Instead of spending the million dollars on getting some model to hoke your product, I’d rather spend that million dollars in the lab trying to figure out why this product is good and how we can make it better” (Goldman).

Dr. Goldman is regarded as a world-renowned authority in a variety of fields, including laser surgery, resurfacing, liposculpture, sclerotherapy, laser closure of varicose veins, dermal fillers, Botox®, Dysport®, Xeomin® therapy, photodynamic therapy, and photorejuvenation. He is the American Society for Dermatologic Surgery’s Director.
