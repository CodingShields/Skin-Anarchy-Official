Skin Health Checkup with Social Media’s Favorite Derm ft. Dr. Lindsey Zubritsky, Founder of DERMGURU, Podcast Summary

In this episode, Ekta sits with Dr. Lindsey Zubritsky, founder of DERMGURU, to discuss the essential needs of everyday skincare products to promote long-term skin health, including the answers to some of the frequent questions within dermatology practice.

“I knew I wanted to specifically do dermatology; I was just always fascinated by skin, and the ability to look at something and know the diagnosis immediately.” (Zubritsky).

Dr. Zubritsky takes pride in brands that promote health over wealth and cares about catering to the average consumer she serves daily. It is for that exact reason she became an ambassador for SunBum, due to their unique products and promotion of sunscreen protection for all.

“I would never work with a brand that I didn’t 100% fully stand behind their mission, their products, and what they put out there. It’s really important to me that when I decide to partner with a brand that we’re on the same page.” (Zubritsky).

Social media drastically changes the consumer world, especially in beauty, and Dr. Zubritsky understands why the science behind a brand’s product is just as important as its results. It is why she is the DERMGURU!

“I think it’s important that we are medical doctors, and we don’t end up like some TV doctors who promote things that are actually dangerous for you.” (Zubritsky).
