Rose Fernandez, CEO of Algenist Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Rose Fernandez, the CEO of Algenist. Fernandez has been in sales and marketing for the beauty/skincare industry for over 20 years. She spent more time in the business and started to love the business side of it, and was impressed with the women leadership. She worked her way up the ranks through a lot of different positions and environments. She joined
Algenist in September of 2016, she remembers being so impressed with the clinicals and claims that came from the story of how the brand and it’s products came to be. Algenist is an algae based skincare brand with numerous awards and praises from many reputable sources. “What’s so powerful about micro-algae, it’s so unknown even to this day what we can do with micro-
algae. It keeps delivering in ways that I don’t think we know” (Fernandez).
