Tuning Into The Beauty Vibe Ft. Noelle Shine, CEO of VIBE PARTNERS, Podcast Summary

In this episode, Ekta sits down with Noelle Shine, CEO of VIBE PARTNERS to discuss her journey for the last 30 years in the beauty industry with the original managing team of Charlotte Tilbury Beauty. While there, Noelle would develop global customer distribution and trading strategies for the first three years of its founding.

“Trading doesn’t necessarily only mean distribution, it could be figuring out what you want to be famous for because that can become a founding stone for you.” (Shine).

Since her days at Charlotte T. Beauty, Noelle has worked with managing teams of over 25 indie beauty brands, such as Trinny London, Code 8, and VIEVE. But what made her stand out the most among other beauty experts is her determination to understand the consumer and their perspective in marketing a product.

“Sometimes you may just want to create a brand that’s very niche but if you want to be able to scale your brand, you do have to appeal to enough people that care enough and that is truly the power of “why are you doing this” at any start of any idea.” (Shine).

Noelle understands social media plays a massive role in how a brand’s audience sees them and takes hold of that to advance marketing strategies. She believes that the media plays a part in the consumer and company relationship as much as the product itself because it allows a brand to connect with clients faster and on a personal level!

“Social media, such as TikTok, is posing a true test of proving if people see and know it’s you and they stand and connect with that aspect. Each channel has a different purpose but if you’re able to be your own voice, and can get others to be your voice, this can be very important.” (Shine).
