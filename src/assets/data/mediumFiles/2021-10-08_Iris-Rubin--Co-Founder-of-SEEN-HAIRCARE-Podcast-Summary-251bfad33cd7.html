Iris Rubin, Co-Founder of SEEN HAIRCARE Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Dr. Iris Rubin, Co-Founder of SEEN HAIRCARE. Iris is a board certified dermatologist with laser and cosmetic fellowship training. She graduated Harvard Medical School and has a history of working at Children’s National Medical Center to help treat children and babies with skin defects as a laser surgeon.

Iris never thought she would be launching a haircare line but her passion towards creating products that help your hair and your skin shines through. She noticed that she was still getting acne well into her 30’s and found that the correlation between the two were because of the hair products she was using, which is an issue that is more common than many might think. “It’s not about layering on more and more products but about using the right products” (Iris Rubin).
