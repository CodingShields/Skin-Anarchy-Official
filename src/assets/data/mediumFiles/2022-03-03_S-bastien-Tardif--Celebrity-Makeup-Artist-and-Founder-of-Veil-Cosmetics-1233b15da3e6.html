Sébastien Tardif, Celebrity Makeup Artist and Founder of Veil Cosmetics

This week Ekta talks to Sébastien Tardif, Celebrity Makeup Artist and Founder of Veil Cosmetics. Tardif’s work has taken him to New York, London, Los Angeles, Hong Kong, Tokyo and beyond. “I thought, what could I do to get into fashion? Get into the eye of the storm, the middle of the excitement, while also being productive and having a contribution. That’s when the idea of being a makeup artist dawned on me.” (Tardif).

For more than 25 years, he has led innovation for many global luxury brands, and has beautified celebrities including Naomi Campbell, Nelly Furtado, Jane Fonda, Olivia Palermo and Glen Close to name a few. He has led makeup direction for more than 100 fashion week shows in New York and internationally, and his signature illuminated makeup has appeared in Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Elle magazines and more.

“You need to do your homework prior to arriving at the job. You need to know who’s going to be your client, who you’re doing makeup on and try to get there earlier, so you can set up properly.” (Tardif). He is the creator of Veil Cosmetics, renowned for its textural superiority, and a cult favorite amongst beauty experts and professionals.
