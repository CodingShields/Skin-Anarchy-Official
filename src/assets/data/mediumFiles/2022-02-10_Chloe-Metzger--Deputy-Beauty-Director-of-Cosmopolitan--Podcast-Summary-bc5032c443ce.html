Chloe Metzger, Deputy Beauty Director of Cosmopolitan, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Chloe Metzger, the Deputy Beauty Director of Cosmopolitan. Metzger oversees the editorial content and growth strategy of the skin, makeup, and hair space on digital. She brings nearly a decade of beauty expertise from working with brands like Allure, Marie Claire, Health, and StyleCaster, and fosters a lifelong skincare obsession. Chloe ended up in
editorial in a non-traditional way. She always knew she wanted to work in magazines and editorial. When she was a freshman in high school she applied to be a writer at a music website and was hired. She started writing about music bands and going to shows and interviewing the performers, all at fifteen years old. Her introduction to this industry was younger than most but
nonetheless she has had the drive for editorial since childhood. “Chloe Metzger is the deputy beauty director at Cosmopolitan, obsessively writing about new makeup launches, the best hair products (curly girl here), and the skincare formulas that really work for every skin type.” (Cosmopolitan.com).
