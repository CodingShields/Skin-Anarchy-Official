Revela Is Leading the Way Towards Reversing Hair Loss with Their Hair Revival Serum ft. Dr. David Zhang, PhD, Co-Founder & CSO at Revela, Podcast Summary

In this episode, Ekta sits with Dr. David Zhang, Ph.D., Co-Founder & CSO at Revela, to discuss the brand’s breakthrough discovery of hair growth regimes that are taking the cosmetology industry by storm. Dr. Zhang always had a passion for innovating modern medicine with assistance with cancer therapy development as an immunologist.

“I think Revela’s very interesting from a lot of other brands. We were founded by a team of scientists and engineers. I myself, was an immunologist, spending my entire life developing these cancer therapeutics.” (Zhang).

Fortunately, Dr. Zhang uses his skills to formulate the Revela Hair Loss Serum, which ingredients work to help regrow and regenerate the hair of the scalp.

“We did our research and really tried to focus on ingredients that are out there that currently do work and those ended up as ingredients for our formula. Our process is really focused on finding key ingredients that can help with hair loss and hair thinning.” (Zhang).

What makes Revela stand out amongst other brands in the beauty industry is their constant drive to revolutionize their formulations with ingredients that are not only clean but give results through all sections of beauty.

“We’re really focused on trying to find better ingredients to make more and better products, not just in skincare or haircare but also all around the world of wellness.” (Zhang).
