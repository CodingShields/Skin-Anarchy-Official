Korean Skincare Infused Makeup, SIIA Cosmetics is Leading the Way to Everyday Glam ft. Megan Rein, Co-Founder of SIIA Cosmetics

This Monday, join Ekta as she sits down with Megan Rein, Co-Founder of SIIA Cosmetics & President of Strategy at Rose De Nuit, to discuss her experience in international business and industrial development within the beauty industry. Megan always loved beauty as she could dive into makeup as a theater, which led to her receiving a county-wide makeup award. But it was during her time studying abroad as an undergraduate at USF that she could dive into her passion for international and industrial development.

“I was playing with makeup from when I was very little. Then when I pursued more of my professional career, I put that aside for a couple of years. I studied international development, and I ended up working in an international program management and started traveling all over the world for a big corporation.” (Rein).

Although Megan had wonderful opportunities to travel worldwide, her skin had trouble adapting to the several environments she would encounter. Thus, she would try every brand she could think of and would have little to no results.

“At the time, I was traveling to places like India and the Middle East, and Europe. And all these different climates. I would have the worst time traveling around. Havin breakouts, my products just wouldn’t work with me going to all these different places. My skin was just so irritated.” (Rein).

From this experience, Megan would go on to promote SIIA to produce products that gave innovative results across the border lines of climate skin irritation.

“I was frustrated and wanted products to work as hard as I was working.” (Rein).
