Episode 383: Jan Marini’s Countdown To The Holidays!

In this holiday episode of Skincare Anarchy, Ekta is joined by Jan Marini! Jan Marini is the Founder and Chairman of Jan Marini Skin Research Inc. Working alongside some of the best physicians and research scientists to create solutions to some of the most common skincare concerns we face. Marini is an avid product researcher, adding much weight to the word of her advice and expertise.

Tune in to this episode to get industry advice on achieving holiday-ready skin. There’s five weeks until Christmas and the new year is just around the corner. If you have any skincare concerns that you want to address before your holiday parties and gatherings, this episode is for you! From tired skin to acne and rosacea, Marini gives the 411 on how to handle them.

Learn about skin triggers and warning for how you approach cultivating a skincare routine; and get in on tips, tricks, and product picks to lock in your best skin for this holiday season with your front seat to Marinin’s skincare recommendations!
