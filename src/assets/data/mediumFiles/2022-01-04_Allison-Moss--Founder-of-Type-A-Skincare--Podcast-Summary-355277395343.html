Allison Moss, Founder of Type:A Skincare, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Allison Moss, the Founder of Type:A Skincare. “There’s something
nourishing about skincare, it’s nourishing for your skin but it’s also very ‘feel good’ in a different way than cosmetics or fragrance. It’s very personal, you’re doing something for yourself, and I always loved that.” (Moss). Moss has 22 years of prior marketing and management experience
working with some of the biggest beauty companies in the business. Her passion for wanting to bring cleaner, safer beauty & personal care products to market brought her to where she is today,
Founder & CEO of Type:A Brands. Since launching Type:A Brands three years ago, Allison has created award-winning, non-toxic, high-performance body care items. Allison has led the company to develop an impactful foundation and immense growth. This includes the hero product, a game-changing aluminum-free deodorant, being awarded a patent for it’s exclusive
sweat-activated technology that delivers best-in-class odor and wetness protection. Type:A Brands is a certified B Corporation, carbon neutral, cruelty free, woman-owned and all products are made in the USA.
