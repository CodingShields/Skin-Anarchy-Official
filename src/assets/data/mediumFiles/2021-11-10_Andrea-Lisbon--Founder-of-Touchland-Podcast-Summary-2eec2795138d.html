Andrea Lisbon, Founder of Touchland Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Andrea Lisbon, the Founder of Touchland. Touchland is a brand that makes Hand Sanitizer that elevates everyday experiences. Touchland combines their sleek and functional packaging with a non-stick, moisturizing, and luxurious formula. The formula
contains simplistic ingredients, including a lot of natural ingredients and essential oils, only stuff that is needed and no extra chemicals that could harm skin. Their brand is cruelty-free, gluten-free, paraben-free, and vegan as well. It’s important to Andrea as well as her company that their formula was skin forward and it’s centered around taking care of the hands and the skin. An achievement Touchland has received is they’re the first hand sanitizer that has received an “Allure Best Of Beauty” award.
