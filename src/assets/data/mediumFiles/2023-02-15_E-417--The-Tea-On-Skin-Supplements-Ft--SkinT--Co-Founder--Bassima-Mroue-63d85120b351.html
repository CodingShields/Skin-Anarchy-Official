E.417: The Tea On Skin Supplements Ft. SkinTē Co-Founder, Bassima Mroue

In this podcast episode hosted by Dr. Ekta of Skincare Anarchy, Bassima Mroue, the co-founder of SkinTē, a company that produces supplements for skin health, is interviewed about the use of supplements for improving skin health.

Mroue explains that SkinTē was founded in response to the lack of effective and high-quality supplements for skin health. She believes that many supplements on the market contain low-quality ingredients or are not formulated in a way that maximizes their benefits for the skin. SkinTē’s supplements are designed to provide a comprehensive range of nutrients that support skin health from the inside out.

The conversation covers various topics related to supplements for skin health, such as the difference between supplements and topical skincare products, the importance of choosing high-quality ingredients, and the potential benefits of using supplements for specific skin concerns, such as aging or acne.

Mroue emphasizes the importance of consulting with a healthcare professional before starting any supplement regimen, as individual needs and health conditions can vary. She also highlights the importance of using supplements in conjunction with a healthy diet and lifestyle, as they cannot replace proper nutrition and skincare practices.

Mroue explains that SkinTē’s supplements are formulated with high-quality, clinically-studied ingredients that are carefully selected to target specific skin concerns. For example, the SkinTē Age Defense supplement contains ingredients such as astaxanthin and CoQ10, which are antioxidants that can help protect the skin from oxidative stress and the signs of aging.

Dr. Ekta and Mroue also discuss the role of vitamins and minerals in skin health, as well as the potential benefits of using supplements for improving conditions such as rosacea and eczema.

Mroue emphasizes that while supplements can be a helpful addition to a skincare routine, they are not a replacement for a healthy diet and lifestyle. She recommends that individuals work with a healthcare professional to determine which supplements may be most beneficial for their unique needs and concerns.

Overall, the podcast provides a helpful overview of the use of supplements for skin health and offers insights into how SkinTē is working to provide high-quality supplements that can support overall skin health.
