Jenna Rosenstein, Beauty Director At BAZAAR.COM, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Jenna Rosenstein, the Beauty Director at BAZAAR.com. Jenna attended
NYU and held internships at Women’s Wear Daily, Bloomingdale’s, Harper’s BAZAAR, and Allure. Her first job was at Allure where she was the Beauty Assistant but left after three years as the Senior Beauty Editor, she spent a couple of months at Refinery29 with the same title which
had her overseeing branded content before landing back at BAZAAR. She loved Journalism and throughout her high school experience she had her eyes on NYU’s Journalism Program. It never
occurred to her that beauty journalists existed and when she found that out she knew she found her calling. She always loved to write and found that she succeeded mainly in English while in school and she also had this deep love for beauty and what different makeup and skincare could do for your confidence and found herself falling into a job that was her passion.
