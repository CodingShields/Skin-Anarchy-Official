Sahar and Sahar, Co-Founders of Sundree Skincare, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Sahar and Sahar, the Co-Founders of Sundree Skincare. The girls
acknowledge that skincare not only is about your physical appearance but also about your
internal confidence and mental health. To them, that was a starting point of their journey into this industry. They both talk about how their skin types are very different and they wanted to figure out formulas that could work for any skin type. Sahar has very combination skin (oily/dry) and is
very acne prone while the other Sahar has pretty oily skin. When creating their line and their
products they decided to include CBD in the formula because they found that it actually worked and enhanced both of their faces as well as having great benefits on the skin like being anti-inflammatory and hydrating. “What started out as just a couple of skincare-obsessed friends swapping homemade serums has flourished into a high-efficacy skincare line. We believe that
happy skin helps you feel happy within and vice versa — happiness on the inside gives you a natural glow on the outside. Made in sunny California, Sundree products are the manifestation of a shared vision between Sahar Sanjar Dejban and Sahar Vazin Nezhad, two besties who were looking to simplify their skincare routines and share the countless benefits of CBD with friends like you.” (sundree.com).
