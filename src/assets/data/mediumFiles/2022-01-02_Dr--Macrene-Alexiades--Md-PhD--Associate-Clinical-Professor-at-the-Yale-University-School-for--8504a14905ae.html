Dr. Macrene Alexiades, Md PhD, Associate Clinical Professor at the Yale University School for Medicine, and Founder of Macrene Activities, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Dr. Macrene Alexiades, Md Phd, Associate Clinical Professor at the Yale University School for Medicine, and Founder of Macrene Activities. Dr Alexiades is a world renowned medical professional who focuses on science based skincare and beauty. In the episode Ekta and Macrene discuss everything from the lies of the clean beauty industry to how you can be ingesting toxic micro plastics. Alexiades also talks about her brand, Macrene Activities which launched in 2009 with her High Performance Anti-Aging Face Cream. The cream was the first to use hyaluronic acid as a ingredient. Dr. Macrene Alexiades discussed how to got into school at such a young age on the podcast as well, and she recants the interesting story of how she got a job during her freshman year at Harvard at a professor’s lab that she read about years earlier. Lastly, she also mentioned all about toxins in skincare and what clean skincare actually means. You can check out here findings right here. (https://macreneactives.com/pages/clean-beauty-article)
