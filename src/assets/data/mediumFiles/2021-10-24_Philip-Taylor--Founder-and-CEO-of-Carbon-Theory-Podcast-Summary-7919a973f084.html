Philip Taylor, Founder and CEO of Carbon Theory Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Philip Taylor, The Founder and CEO of Carbon Theory. Taylor’s mission with this brand was to create an anti-breakout brand, something that is easy to use and effective for acne. This brand is a global leader in the clean performance skincare category. Taylor wanted to expand the field of normal products that teenagers and adults alike could use,
to avoid steps such as Accutane and other steroidal products that could cause/expose them to other long term harm. He wanted a “one stop shop product” which perfectly explains Carbon Theory’s cleansing bar which he created himself in his kitchen just a few years ago. It’s effective to use and easy to understand. “We’re not here to pretend breakouts don’t exist, or to promise
you a quick fix. We’re here to have honest conversations about skincare, to share your personal experiences, and to provide products to keep breakouts at bay and boost your confidence.” (carbontheory.com).
