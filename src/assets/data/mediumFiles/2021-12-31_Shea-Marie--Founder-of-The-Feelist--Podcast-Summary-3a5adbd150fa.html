Shea Marie, Founder of The Feelist, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Shea Marie, Founder of The Feelist. Entrepreneur and Creative Director Shea Marie has developed a devoted following of millions, and her position as one of the world’s top influencers has landed her jobs with the largest beauty, health, wellness and fashion brands.
When she was creating The Feelist, she wanted to create something that gave the results but also looked and felt luxurious. She first entered the world of clean beauty after discovering her own
sensitivities to many of the harmful ingredients contained in skincare and wellness products. Her position in the industry has allowed her to try nearly every product, treatment and beauty/health offering on the market. Her initial interest in plant-based skincare came about as she searched for
a more holistic and natural approach to her wellness routine. But navigating the “natural” landscape was tricky, most products seemed small batch and untrustworthy, with no branding or reliable history. Shea knew that if she was looking for beautiful and trustworthy products in the space, others would be too and set out to build a brand whose mission is to create clean and functional products that truly make you feel like your best self.
