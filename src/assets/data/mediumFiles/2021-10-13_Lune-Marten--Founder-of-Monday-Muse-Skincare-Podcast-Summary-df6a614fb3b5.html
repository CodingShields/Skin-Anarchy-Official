Lune Marten, Founder of Monday Muse Skincare Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Lune Marten, the Founder of Monday Muse Skincare. Marten has a Bachelor’s and a Master’s degree and also acquired her diploma in Skin Care Formulating to create her products. Monday Muse is her brand that she started to address the concerns of skin conditions like Scarring, Rosacea, Cystic Acne, etc. Marten talks about her lifelong struggle with acne and the brand was born through her own experience. She strived to create products that delivered results because she found in her skincare trials throughout her youth and young adult life that many products and brands over promise yet under deliver. Marten wanted her brand to be elegant, the packaging, the formulas and everything in between to be more aesthetically pleasing to customers than some of the stuff she used when she was going through her journey.
