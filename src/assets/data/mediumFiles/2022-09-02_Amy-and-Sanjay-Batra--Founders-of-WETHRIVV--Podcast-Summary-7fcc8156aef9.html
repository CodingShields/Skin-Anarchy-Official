Amy and Sanjay Batra, Founders of WETHRIVV, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta sits down with Amy and Sanjay Batra, Founders of WETHRIVV, to discuss the journey of one of the leading companies in the space of aesthetic skin care and post-procedure recovery. Dr. Batra and Amy work tirelessly to study the benefits of aesthetic procedures by utilizing natural products such as platelet-rich plasma, a signature ingredient of their line. Together they have created a brand that shows results and breaks the barriers of medicine and consumer needs in the skin health and care space.

“What we did here in our formula is we looked tirelessly at the literature, the non-clinical and clinical data to find not only the right ingredients but to put them in at the right concentration to create a very elegant formula!” (S. Batra).

The Batras are well known for their achievements in not only skincare, but Dr. Batra’s brilliant study of hair loss and hair follicles created the serum known today as Revive. But what is tremendous about this product is that Dr. Batra and Amy made sure that this product would be free of drugs that cause significant side effects like most brands market to the public.

“We wanted a drug free solution you know it was very important for us to have something that didn’t have side effects, like the two things that the FDA has approved to this date one being minoxidil and the other being finasteride.” (S. Batra).

The demanding work and determination from the Batras have revolutionized the idea of skin and beauty health for brands and their consumers. They continue to innovate their line to keep up with what is new and safe to ensure every client has the results they deserve!

“Our mission is very simple. We pay in facial aesthetics, dermatology, retentive medicine, and hair loss. And we want to create the best products possible and that’s what we have done tirelessly.” (S. Batra).
