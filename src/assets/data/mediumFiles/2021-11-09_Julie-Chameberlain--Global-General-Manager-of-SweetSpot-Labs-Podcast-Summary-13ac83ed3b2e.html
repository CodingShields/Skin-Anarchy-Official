Julie Chameberlain, Global General Manager of SweetSpot Labs Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Julie Chameberlain, the Global General Manager of SweetSpot Labs. Julie is from Canada and started her career with L’Oreal in sales and eventually moved up the ranks and her last position before leaving was a General Manager for NYX Makeup. Julie talks about her love for beauty but more importantly the emotional side of it, she loves how internally confident beauty and skincare make people feel and how connected they feel to themselves when they feel at their best. SweetSpot Labs is based on fearlessness and innovation and was founded back in 2003. The brand’s eco-friendly packaging and clean ingredients that actually give results
is what makes them stand out from other feminine care brands. Their line is driven by science and a leader in normalizing feminine care.
