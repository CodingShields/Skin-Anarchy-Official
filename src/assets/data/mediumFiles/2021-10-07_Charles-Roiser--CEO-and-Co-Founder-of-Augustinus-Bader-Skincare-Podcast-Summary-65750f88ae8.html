Charles Roiser, CEO and Co-Founder of Augustinus Bader Skincare Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Charles Rosier, the Co-Founder and CEO of Augustinus Bader Skincare. Rosier works along-side Dr. Bader in creating and managing their brand and the products they release. Augustinus Bader is an expert in stem-cell science and technology and remains a professor to this day. Charles and Augustinus met through a mutual friend about nine years ago
and when they met, Augustinus was trying out formulas to help his friend’s four year old daughter who had suffered second and third degree burns. Augustinus created the hydrogel which has been a revolutionary product in the skincare game as well as the plastics/dermatology medical field.

This brand is truly a leader in skincare, they’ve received approximately sixty-two awards in a little under four years. Charles goes on to talk about his relationship with skincare and how his background is in finance and science and not exactly the beauty industry but he and Augustinus love the attention and adoration the brand is receiving by the community.
