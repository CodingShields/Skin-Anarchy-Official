Pauline and Johnny Paterson, Co-Founders of Dr. PAWPAW, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Pauline and Johnny Paterson, Co-Founders of Dr. PAWPAW.
Dr. PAWPAW is a brand that specializes in treating eczema, they are a clean skincare/beauty
brand. It was created back in 2013 when Johnny and Pauline’s daughter was diagnosed with eczema. It was then that the need for a natural alternative to the normal steroid prescription creams turned a want into a must. The husband and wife duo decided to create a multipurpose balm that could be used on lips, skin and hair. Both Johnny and Pauline envisioned a range of products that would help families everyday, an affordable product that all the family could use — from Grandmas to new born babies. Johnny has an extensive background in PR and ran a PR agency for a portion of his career. “All our tubes are 100% recyclable. We strive to ensure all our ingredients are ethically and sustainably sourced, we have strong beliefs that all our products should be cruelty free and certify that all our Dr.PAWPAW products are PETA approved.” (drpawpaw.com).
