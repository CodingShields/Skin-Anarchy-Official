Kym Douglass, Beauty Expert for The Ellen Show, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Kym Douglas, a beauty industry veteran and a beauty expert for The Ellen Show. Kym has this fire within her that she has not let go of at any point in her life, always encouraged by her family to chase her dreams. Kym is a comedian, television host, best-selling author, and lifestyle expert most recently known for her role on Hallmark Channel’s
Emmy®-nominated show “Home & Family”’ where she is often called upon to co-host as well.
For the past 16 years, she has been entertaining viewers with her antics on the #1 rated daytime “The Ellen DeGeneres Show.”Kym has a broad and evolving fanbase that has taken followers and viewers through her roles on The Bold and The Beautiful, In Living Color, and L.A. Law.
Kym and her husband, Jerry Douglas, (John Abbott, The Young and the Restless) have been married for 36 years and have one son, Hunter. After battling stage 3 breast cancer, Kym has been dedicated to helping women live more inspired and beautiful lives, while aiming to ignite change within the beauty industry with her mission to bring safer, cleaner beauty to all. With a playful heart she always manages to add just the right dose of humor as she informs, inspires, and encourages her fans and followers daily.
