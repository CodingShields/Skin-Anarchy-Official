Gillian Gorman Round, CEO of Kjaer Weis Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to the CEO of Kjaer Weis, Gillian Gorman Round. Round talks about her outstanding and diverse experience working with brands like L’Oreal, Lancôme, Revlon, Yves Saint-Laurent and of course, Kjaer Weis. She’s been in the business for over 20 years and has a well rounded background. Her story is a full circle given that she began in beauty and then moved on to do some media &amp; marketing projects and then landed herself back into beauty.

Round talks about her unique skill set and how her knowledge across different channels has allowed her to diversify her experience and use her skills in different places and occupations.
