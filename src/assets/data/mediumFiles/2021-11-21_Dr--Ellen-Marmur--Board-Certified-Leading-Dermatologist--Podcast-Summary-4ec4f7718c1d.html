Dr. Ellen Marmur, Board Certified Leading Dermatologist, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Dr. Ellen Marmur, a Board Certified Leading Dermatologist. Dr.
Marmur is a leading dermatologist in New York City and has been the Chief of Dermatologic Surgery at Mount Sinai Medical Center. Her expertise is in Skin Cancer and Surgery, Mohs Surgery, Reconstructive Surgery, Women’s Health Dermatology, and Cosmetic Surgery. She is
also a Master of Aesthetic Procedures like Neuromodulators, Fillers, Lasers, Body Sculpting,
Liposuction and more. She decided to follow her dream and go back to school and become a doctor, she was originally on the path for OB-GYN but at the last moment she took a course on Dermatology and was hooked ever since. She’s a very respected Medical Professional and has done revolutionary procedures and received accolades throughout her twenty plus year career.
She;s also been featured in many media outlets such as Cosmopolitan, Vogue, Allure, Dr. Oz, The Today Show, and many more.
