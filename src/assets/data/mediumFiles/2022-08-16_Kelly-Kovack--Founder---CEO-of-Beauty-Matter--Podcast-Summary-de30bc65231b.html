Kelly Kovack, Founder & CEO of Beauty Matter, Podcast Summary

This week Ekta talks to Kelly Kovack, the Founder & CEO of Beauty Matter. Kelly has decades of experience and is a beauty brand expert. Her years of work both inside and outside the beauty industry have shaped how we consumers should approach beauty.

“My career did not start in media. I was a couple of decades on the brand side of the beauty industry — Building and advising brands and starting some brands of my own. I kind of fell into the beauty industry; I was in fashion. I was a part of the first executive team of bliss and was responsible for starting the catalog.” (Kovack).

While starting and working on the publication, Kelly strives to tell stories that no one else is. Give the truth on all things beauty industry.

“We need to do this for the consumers out there — there’s a lot of information out there, and there’s a lot of misinformation out there. If we’re not, as an industry, ready to have those conversations, then we’re not really serving our consumers” (Kovack).

Kelly’s work in the beauty industry is what consumers need, the unfiltered truth!

“Always be learning — don’t stay in a position that isn’t right.” (Kovack).
